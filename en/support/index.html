Support Centers

Support centers are free services that are run by various governmental and volunteer organizations, and they provide information and other support for problems in daily life that are related to COVID-19 but not medical emergencies. Some of them will also offer interpretation services if you need help calling a Japanese-language COVID-19 hotline.
